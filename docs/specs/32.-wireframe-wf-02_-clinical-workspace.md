# VistA Evolved
Wireframe Specifications
WF-02: Clinical Workspace
Document 2 of 16 | ~92 Pages | Patient Chart + Clinical Reminders + Consults + Medicine Procedures + Dietetics + Mental Health + Nursing + Social Work
March 2026 | The largest workspace in VistA Evolved. The Clinical Workspace unifies all patient-facing clinical functions into a single coherent workspace. Sub-modules accessible via horizontal tab bar within the workspace: Chart (core patient record), Reminders (clinical reminders + consults), Procedures (medicine procedures), Nutrition (dietetics), Mental Health, Nursing, Social Work. Each sub-module tab may have its own nested sub-navigation. All pages reference WF-01 templates (Worklist, Detail View, Form, Dashboard, Report, Config, Calendar). All pages render inside the WF-01 App Shell with Patient Banner visible.
