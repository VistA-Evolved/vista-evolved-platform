WF-03: Pharmacy Workspace
Document 3 of 16 | ~72 Pages | Outpatient Rx + Inpatient Meds + BCMA + Controlled Substances + Pharmacy Config + CMOP + ECME + Ward Stock
March 2026 | The second-largest workspace. The Pharmacy Workspace unifies all medication management functions: outpatient prescription processing (Vol 5), inpatient medication ordering and dispensing (Vol 5B), barcode medication administration at bedside (Vol 5C BCMA), controlled substance tracking with DEA accountability (Vol 5D), pharmacy configuration and national drug file management (Vol 5E), consolidated mail-order pharmacy (Vol 5F CMOP), electronic pharmacy claims (Vol 5F ECME), and ward stock inventory (Vol 5F AR/WS). All pages reference WF-01 templates and render inside the App Shell.

BC-01
BCMA MAR (Medication Administration Record)
Worklist (WF-01 5.1) SPECIALIZED
Full-width table: rows = scheduled medications, columns = time slots (0000-2359 in administration windows). Cell states: Due (amber clock), Given (green check + time + initials), Held (yellow pause + reason), Refused (red X + reason), Omitted (gray dash + reason), Not Given (empty). Patient header: compact banner + last vitals. Bottom: PRN section expandable.
PSB BCMA data via ZVE BCMA MAR wrapper. PHARMACY PATIENT #55 for orders.
Primary nursing medication view. Time slot cells clickable to document administration. Overdue cells (past due time, not yet given) pulsate amber. Upcoming (within 1 hour) highlighted. 24-hour view default, scrollable to 48h/72h. Print: generates formatted MAR for paper chart backup.
BC-02
Scan Administration
Form (WF-01 5.3) TABLET-OPTIMIZED
Per previous detailed wireframe (WF-01 Section 6): (1) Scan Patient prompt (large barcode icon, --color-info background until verified), (2) After patient verified (green confirmation), MAR appears with Due medications highlighted, (3) Select medication to administer, (4) Scan Medication Barcode prompt, (5) Five Rights verification result (GREEN match or RED mismatch), (6) If match: confirm administration (Given/Held/Refused buttons). (7) For non-scannable: manual override with reason documentation + pharmacist notification.
ZVE BCMA SCAN wrapper -> PSB BCMA data.
FIVE RIGHTS ENGINE: (1) Right Patient (wristband barcode = system patient), (2) Right Drug (med barcode NDC = ordered drug NDC), (3) Right Dose (scanned quantity matches ordered dose), (4) Right Route (verified against order), (5) Right Time (within administration window). ALL FIVE GREEN -> 'Given' enabled. ANY RED -> 'Given' DISABLED + mismatch banner. Manual override: documented exception with mandatory reason, generates alert to pharmacy.
BC-03
PRN Medication Administration
Form (WF-01 5.3)
PRN medication list: Drug, Dose, Route, Frequency Limit (e.g. Q4H PRN), Last Given (time + hours since), Available Now (Y/N based on frequency). Pre-administration assessment: pain score (if analgesic), symptom assessment (if other PRN). Scan workflow same as BC-02 after selection. Post-administration: effectiveness reassessment reminder scheduled (e.g. 30 min after pain med).
ZVE BCMA PRN wrapper.
Frequency limit enforcement: if medication given within minimum interval, 'Administer' button DISABLED with countdown timer showing when next dose available. Pre-assessment required before PRN analgesics (pain score 0-10). Post-effectiveness reassessment: auto-reminder in 30-60 minutes (configurable).
BC-04
Due List
Worklist (WF-01 5.1)
List of all medications due NOW for all patients on the nurse's assignment. Columns: Patient+Room, Drug, Dose, Route, Due Time, Minutes Until/Past Due, Status. Sorted by due time (most overdue first). Actions: 'Go to Patient' launches BC-02 scan workflow with patient pre-selected.
ZVE BCMA DUELIST wrapper.
Cross-patient view: nurse sees all due medications across assigned patients to prioritize. RED rows: overdue >30 minutes. AMBER rows: due within 30 minutes. GREEN rows: upcoming within 1 hour. Allows nurse to plan medication pass efficiently.
BC-05
Missing Dose Tracking
Worklist (WF-01 5.1)
Orders with no administration documented at the scheduled time: Patient, Drug, Scheduled Time, Hours Overdue, Last Known Action (Held? Refused? Nothing documented?). Actions: Investigate (open patient chart), Document Late Give, Document Omission with Reason.
ZVE BCMA MISSING wrapper.
Quality monitoring tool: identifies medications that were ordered but never documented as given, held, or refused. May indicate: medication not available, patient off ward for procedure, documentation gap. Pharmacy and nursing management review this report daily.
BC-06
Administration History
Detail View (WF-01 5.2)
Chronological history of all medication administrations for a patient: Date/Time, Drug, Dose, Route, Status (Given/Held/Refused/Omitted), Administered By (nurse name), Scan Verification (Y/N — was barcode scanned or manual override?), Override Reason (if manual). Filter by date range, drug, status.
BCMA MEDICATION LOG #53.79 via ZVE BCMA HISTORY wrapper.
Audit view: shows every administration event including who, when, what, and whether barcode verification was used. Manual override events highlighted amber for pharmacy review. Export for quality reporting.
BC-07
Infusion Pump Integration
Detail View (WF-01 5.2)
Active IV infusions: Patient, Drug+Solution, Ordered Rate, Actual Rate (from pump if integrated), Volume Infused, Volume Remaining, Time Remaining, Pump Status (Infusing/Stopped/Alarm). Smart pump drug library download status. Auto-programming status.
ZVE BCMA PUMP wrapper -> smart pump API (BD Alaris, ICU Medical Plum 360).
Emerging integration per Device Catalog Rule 8. Smart pump auto-programming: BCMA sends order parameters to pump, eliminating manual pump programming (reduces dosing errors). Drug library: dose limits per drug loaded from pharmacy formulary to pump. Pump alarms surface in BCMA interface.
BC-08 to BC-10
BCMA Virtual Due List, BCMA Reports, BCMA Configuration
Various
BC-08 (Virtual Due List): future medications due within configurable window for pre-pouring/preparation. BC-09 (BCMA Reports): scan rates (% of administrations with barcode verification), override rates, missing dose rates, late administration rates, by ward/nurse/shift. BC-10 (BCMA Config): administration windows, scanner configuration, override reasons, PRN reassessment timers, pump integration settings.
Various ZVE BCMA wrappers.
BC-09 key metric: barcode scan compliance rate — target >95% of all administrations verified by barcode scan. Override rate should be <5%. Reports drive patient safety improvement.

Form (WF-01 5.3)

ALL CS destruction requires witnessed documentation. Partial dose waste (e.g. ordered 2mg morphine from 4mg/mL vial — 2mg administered, 2mg wasted): both nurse (waster) and witness sign. DEA-authorized destruction for expired or recalled CS: follows DEA reverse distribution procedures.
CS-05
CS Inspection / Audit
Form (WF-01 5.3) + Detail View
Inspection form: Date, Inspector(s), Type (Random/Scheduled/For-Cause), Findings per drug: Expected Count (from perpetual inventory) vs Actual Count (physical count). Discrepancy documentation: drug, expected, actual, difference, investigation notes, resolution. Historical inspections list below.
ZVE CS INSPECT wrapper.
Scheduled inspections (monthly/quarterly per VHA policy). Random inspections (unannounced). For-cause inspections (triggered by discrepancy report or suspicion). ANY discrepancy requires documented investigation. Unresolved discrepancies: escalated to Chief Pharmacist and potentially law enforcement.
CS-06
CS Transaction Ledger
Detail View (WF-01 5.2)
Complete transaction history for a specific CS drug: Date/Time, Transaction Type (Received/Dispensed/Returned/Destroyed/Adjusted), Quantity, Running Balance, Patient (for dispensing/returns), Person, Witness. Immutable audit trail. Filter by date range, transaction type.
PSD CS transaction files via ZVE CS LEDGER wrapper.
IMMUTABLE: transactions cannot be edited or deleted (I/L3 per Audit/Irreversibility Matrix). Corrections require compensating transactions (adjustment entries with documented reason). Running balance must ALWAYS match physical vault count. Printable for DEA inspection.
CS-07
CS Prescription Drug Monitoring
Detail View (WF-01 5.2)
State PDMP query for patient: all CS dispensed from any pharmacy (VA and non-VA) within the state. Results: Drug, Prescriber, Pharmacy, Quantity, Date Filled, Days Supply. Red flags: multiple prescribers, multiple pharmacies, high morphine milligram equivalents (MME), overlapping opioid+benzodiazepine.
ZVE CS PDMP wrapper -> State PDMP database API.
Query before prescribing or dispensing CS (mandatory in many states). Red flags prominently displayed. MME calculator: sum of daily opioid doses converted to morphine equivalents (>90 MME = high risk per CDC guideline). PDMP results documented in patient chart.

Report + Config
CS-08 (DEA Reports): DEA-mandated reports (biennial inventory, theft/loss reporting DEA Form 106, destruction records). CS-09 (Accountability Summary): facility-wide CS accountability dashboard (all drugs, balances, discrepancy counts, inspection compliance). CS-10 (CS Config): vault setup, inspection schedules, reorder points, DEA registration, witness requirements, PDMP integration settings.
Various ZVE CS wrappers.
DEA reports: formatted per DEA requirements. Theft/loss: immediate reporting to DEA (Form 106) and VA Police. Configuration: DEA registration number per pharmacy, schedule-specific requirements.
